About Me


I am currently working towards my PhD degree in Griffith University (and jointly with the University of Adelaide) under the supervision of Prof. Chunhua Shen and Prof. Yongsheng Gao. I will graduate at the end of this year, and I am looking for job opportunities now.

My research interests lie broadly in computer vision and deep learning. My current research focuses on image-based / video-based person retrieval, fine-grained image classification, human pose estimation and facial landmark detection.
