About Me


I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in the field of computer vision and deep learning. I intend to submit final thesis before September 2021, and I am looking for job opportunities now.

My research interests lie broadly in computer vision and deep learning. My current research focuses on image-based / video-based person retrieval, fine-grained image classification, human pose estimation and facial landmark detection.
